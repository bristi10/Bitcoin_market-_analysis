# Bitcoin_market-_analysis
Bitcoin market analysis is the study of Bitcoin’s price movements, trading volume, and market trends to understand its future direction. It involves examining historical data, technical indicators, and market sentiment to identify patterns, potential risks, and investment opportunities in the cryptocurrency market.

# Objectives
> Does performance (PnL, win rate, drawdown proxy) differ between Fear vs Greed days?
> Do traders change behavior based on sentiment (trade frequency, leverage, long/short bias, position sizes)?
> Identify 2–3 segments (examples): high leverage vs low leverage traders answer the question frequent vs infrequent traders consistent winners vs inconsistent traders
> Provide at least 3 insights backed by charts/tables.
